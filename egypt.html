Market access in Egypt
certification by Joe Trades

To obtain market approval in Egypt, NTRA approval is necessary, which includes both national specifications and certain elements of the Radio Equipment Directive (RED) standards.

Approval

Most products entering the Egypt market must meet the relevant requirements of the authorities depending on the product scope. Product safety requirements and regulations are put in place to reduce risks, improve quality of life, and ensure safety during use.


Certification Validity: Permanent


Approval Scheme: NTRA


Lead Time for Approval: 4 Weeks


Local representative required? Yes


Sample Required (Yes/No): No


Certification

Once the NTRA (National Telecom Regulatory Authority) grants approval, products with radio technologies receive permanent market approval in Egypt. This means that the equipment does not need to undergo local testing, nor is it necessary to involve a local representative with the authority.
