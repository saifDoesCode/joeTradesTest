Market access in Saudi Arabia
certification by Joe Trades

Market approval in Saudi Arabia is issued by the Communications, Space & Technology Commission (CST), formerly known as the Communications and Information Technology Commission (CITC). CST certification serves as the basis for the market approval of products with radio technologies in Saudi Arabia.

Approval

The conformity assessment system in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia accepts European documents for hassle-free market access.


Certification Validity: 2 Years (24 months)


Approval Scheme: CST


Lead Time for Approval: 2 Weeks


Local representative required? Yes


Sample Required (Yes/No): No


Certification

A separate national certification regime with its own specifications for testing does not exist in Saudi Arabia. The scope of testing for CST certification is based on the requirements defined by the Radio Equipment Directive (RED). Therefore, tests do not need to be conducted in local laboratories in Saudi Arabia. Relevant tests can be carried out in accredited test laboratories, such as cetecom advanced, which are also recognized for the regulatory testing required by the Radio Equipment Directive. A CST certification is valid for two years. After the certification expires, complete recertification is required.
