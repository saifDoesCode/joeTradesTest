Market access in Sudan
certification by Joe Trades

Market approval process is overseen by the National Telecommunications Corporation (NTC). The process ensures that products meet national standards and regulations before being marketed and sold in the country.

Approval

Manufacturers or their representatives must submit an application to the NTC. This application should include detailed technical documentation, test reports, and other relevant information about the product.


Certification Validity: Permanent


Approval Scheme: NTC


Lead Time for Approval: 5 Weeks


Local representative required? Yes


Sample Required (Yes/No): No


Certification

If the product passes the evaluation and testing phases, the NTC issues a type approval certificate. This certificate allows the product to be legally marketed and sold in Sudan.
